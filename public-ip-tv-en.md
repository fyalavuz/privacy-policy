# Privacy Policy for Public IP TV

**Last Updated:** June 25, 2026

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how "Public IP TV" ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our tvOS application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The guiding principle of our application is simple: **We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.**

## 2. Data Collection
We do not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics.

- **No User Accounts:** You do not need to create an account to use the app.
- **No Tracking:** We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase) or advertising networks.
- **No Personal Data:** We do not ask for, collect, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

## 3. Information Stored on Your Device
Some preferences are saved locally on your Apple TV and never leave your device:

- **Favorites, recently watched channels, and hidden channels** are stored only on your device.
- **Settings** such as your selected country, app language, and refresh interval are stored locally.

You can clear this information at any time from within the app (Settings) or by deleting the app.

## 4. Channel Data and Streams
Channel information and stream links come from public, third-party sources. Public IP TV does not host, store, record, re-stream, or proxy any audio or video; it only references public stream URLs. When you play a channel, your Apple TV connects directly to the third-party stream provider, whose own privacy practices apply.

## 5. Third-Party Services
Since we do not collect data, we do not share data with any third parties. We do not use advertising or analytics services.

## 6. Children's Privacy
The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page or within the application.
