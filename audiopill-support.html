AudioPill Support

A home theater setup checker for Apple TV. Verify your speaker setup by ear — routing, levels, subwoofer crossover, phase, and obvious frequency issues — using guided tests on the screen you actually watch.

Getting Started

  1. Open AudioPill on your Apple TV. It launches to the Home tab.
  2. Select Run Setup Check for the guided, step-by-step walkthrough.
  3. Pick your speaker layout (Stereo through 5.1.4 / 9.1.6), then follow each step: Channel Check, Level Matching, Subwoofer Crossover, Phase Check, and Frequency Sweep.
  4. Review the Results summary and recommendations.

Other Tools

  • Diagnostic Assistant — start from a symptom (weak center, boomy bass, broken surround) and jump to the right test.
  • Tools tab — individual generators: Tone, Sweep, Noise, Channel ID, Phase, Silence, Polarity Ping, Multitone, and the bundled Test Library.
  • Diagram — change layouts and view the speaker map.
  • Settings — default layout/level, visualizer toggle, bundled-file count, and about.

Safety

Test tones, sweeps, and noise can be loud. Start with your receiver volume low and raise it gradually from a safe listening level. AudioPill shows a reminder on first use.

FAQ

Do I need an account or internet connection?

No. There is no account, login, or subscription, and the app works fully offline.

Does it use a microphone or measure my room?

No. AudioPill is a by-ear verification tool. It requests no microphone access and does not claim to replace calibrated measurement microphones, SPL meters, or professional room-measurement systems.

Why does a channel sound wrong or silent?

Use Channel Check to confirm each speaker plays. If a channel is missing, verify your AVR is not forced to stereo/downmix, then check speaker wiring and amp assignment.

Is my data collected?

No. See the privacy policy. Everything stays on your device.
