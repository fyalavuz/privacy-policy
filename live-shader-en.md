# Live Shader Privacy Policy

*Effective date: May 3, 2026*

Live Shader does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Version 1.0 does not collect personal information, usage analytics, diagnostics, advertising identifiers, contact information, location data, photos, audio, or user content.

Live Shader does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies. It does not require an account and does not send app activity to a server.

For support, see [Live Shader Support](live-shader-support.md).
