# Sudoku for Apple TV Support

Sudoku for Apple TV is a native classic sudoku game built for the Siri Remote.

## Controls

- D-pad (or swipe) moves the focused cell on the board.
- Press Select on a cell to open the number picker right next to it.
- In the picker: choose a digit, undo, erase, use a hint, or toggle the box filter.
- Press Menu during play to quit — your game is saved automatically and appears under CONTINUE on the main menu.
- Daily puzzles refresh every day; keep your streak by finishing one daily puzzle per day.

## FAQ

**Is my progress saved?** Yes — quitting mid-game autosaves. Statistics and streaks are stored per profile, locally on your Apple TV.

**Does the game need an internet connection?** No. Everything works fully offline.

## Privacy

Sudoku for Apple TV does not collect personal data in version 1.0. See the [privacy policy](sudoku-tvos-en.md).
