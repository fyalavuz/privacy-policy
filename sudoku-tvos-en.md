# Sudoku for Apple TV Privacy Policy

*Effective date: June 12, 2026*

Sudoku for Apple TV does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Version 1.0 does not collect personal information, usage analytics, diagnostics, advertising identifiers, contact information, location data, photos, audio, or user content. All game progress, statistics, daily streaks, profiles, and settings are stored locally on your Apple TV and never leave the device.

Sudoku for Apple TV does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies. It does not require an account, contains no ads, and does not send app activity to a server.

For support, see [Sudoku for Apple TV Support](sudoku-tvos-support.md).
