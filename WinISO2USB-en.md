# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 11, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how FIRAT YALAVUZ ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our application, WinISO2USB ("the App").

## Data Collection and Usage

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.**

WinISO2USB is a local utility application designed to create bootable Windows USB drives. All operations performed by the App, including reading ISO files and writing to USB drives, occur locally on your device.

- **No Analytics:** We do not use third-party analytics services to track your usage.
- **No Account Required:** You do not need to create an account or log in to use the App.
- **No Internet Access:** The App does not require internet access to function and does not transmit any data to external servers.

## Local Storage

The App may access files on your device (specifically ISO files you select) and write data to the USB drives you select. This access is strictly limited to the files and drives you explicitly choose and authorize through the macOS system dialogs and permission prompts. No data is uploaded or transferred off your device.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data.
