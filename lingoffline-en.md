# Lingoffline Privacy Policy

*Effective date: June 26, 2026*

Lingoffline does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Version 1.0 does not collect personal information, usage analytics, diagnostics, advertising identifiers, contact information, location data, photos, audio, or user content.

Lingoffline works offline. Your source and target language choices are stored only on your device. The app does not require an account and does not send app activity to a server.

Lingoffline uses the system text-to-speech engine to pronounce phrases and the system clipboard when you copy a phrase. These actions happen on your device.

Lingoffline does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies.

For support, see [Lingoffline Support](lingoffline-support.md).
