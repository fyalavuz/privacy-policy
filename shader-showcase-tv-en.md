# Shader Showcase TV Privacy Policy

*Effective date: May 3, 2026*

Shader Showcase TV does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Version 1.0 does not collect personal information, usage analytics, diagnostics, advertising identifiers, contact information, location data, photos, audio, or user content.

Shader Showcase TV does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies. It does not require an account and does not send app activity to a server.

For support, see [Shader Showcase TV Support](shader-showcase-tv-support.md).
