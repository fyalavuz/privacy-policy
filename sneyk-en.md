Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-06

Sneyk is an offline snake game for Apple TV. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Collection
- No personal data is collected.
- No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.

Local Storage
Sneyk stores the following settings only on your device:
- High score
- Difficulty level
- Sound on/off preference
- Wall pass-through and color mode preferences

This information stays on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Third Parties
The app does not share data with third parties.

Children's Privacy
Sneyk does not collect data from children.

Changes
If we update this policy, we will post the new version here.

Contact
For privacy questions, use the developer contact information listed on the App Store.
