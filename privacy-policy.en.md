# Privacy Policy
Last Updated: 2025-12-25

This application is a media player. It does not host content and does not require accounts.

## Data We Collect
We do not collect personal information. The application does not include analytics or advertising SDKs.

## Your Data
Playlists, credentials, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not receive or store them on our servers.

## Third-Party Services
When you add a stream or playlist, your device connects directly to the provider you entered. Their servers may log your IP address or usage. Please review their privacy policies.

## Data Retention
Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the application.

## Security
We use reasonable measures to protect data stored on your device, but no method is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy
This application is not intended for children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the "Last Updated" date.
